# uBank Marketing Design Guide & Content Strategy

## 1. Visual Identity System for Marketing
This guide translates the app's digital aesthetic into print and marketing materials (posters, banners, social media).

### **Brand Personality**
*   **Premium**: Clean, uncluttered, high-end financial feel.
*   **Trustworthy**: Stable, secure, transparent.
*   **Community-Focused**: Warm, inclusive, "Zen".
*   **Visual Metaphor**: Growth, Greenery, Light, Glass.

### **Color Palette (CMYK approximations for Print)**
*   **Primary: Emerald Deep** (The Core Brand)
    *   Digital: `oklch(0.45 0.15 145)` / `#059669`
    *   *Usage*: Backgrounds, Main Headlines, Logo.
    *   *Vibe*: Wealth, Growth, Stability.
*   **Accent: Banana Gold** (The Action Color)
    *   Digital: `oklch(0.85 0.16 85)` / `#DDBC10` (approx)
    *   *Usage*: Buttons, CTAs, Highlights, "New" badges.
    *   *Vibe*: Energy, Happiness, Value.
*   **Secondary: Mint & Sage**
    *   *Usage*: Subtle gradients, secondary backgrounds.
*   **Neutral: Clean White & Deep Forest**
    *   *Usage*: Text, negative space.

### **Typography**
*   **Headlines**: **Outfit** (Font Weight: 800/Black)
    *   *Character*: Modern, geometric, friendly but bold.
    *   *Tracking*: Tighter (-0.02em) for large text.
*   **Body Text**: **Inter** (Font Weight: 400/500)
    *   *Character*: Clean, legible, neutral.

### **Graphic Elements**
*   **Glass Cards**: Translucent white boxes with soft shadows (`backdrop-blur`). Use these to hold text or testimonials on top of complex backgrounds.
*   **Gradients**: Smooth transitions from Emerald to Deep Forest, or Banana to Gold.
*   **Rounded Corners**: All photos and boxes should have significant rounding (`20px+` radius).
*   **"Zen" Glows**: Soft radial glows behind key elements to make them "float".

## 2. Marketing Content Strategy

### **Target Audience**
1.  **Village Banking Groups**: Treasurers, chairs, and members looking for better record-keeping.
2.  **Individuals**: People wanting to track their personal wealth alongside group savings.
3.  **Communities**: Churches, workplaces, neighborhoods.

### **Types of Content to Produce**

#### **A. "Problem/Solution" Educational Posters**
*   *Goal*: Show specific pain points the app solves.
*   *Themes*: Transparency, Speed, Security.

#### **B. "Aspirational" Lifestyle Posters**
*   *Goal*: Sell the *feeling* of being organized and wealthy.
*   *Themes*: Peace of mind, financial freedom, future planning.

#### **C. "Trust & Safety" Informational Posters**
*   *Goal*: Overcome hesitation about digital finance.
*   *Themes*: Biometric security, local data privacy, "Your money stays with you".

#### **D. "How It Works" Walkthroughs**
*   *Goal*: Demystify the tech.
*   *Format*: Step-by-step infographics (1-2-3).

## 3. Poster Concepts & Templates

### **Poster 1: The "Transparency" Pitch**
*   **Headline**: "No More Lost Books."
*   **Subhead**: "Digital Village Banking that everyone can see."
*   **Visual**: A split screen. Left side: A messy paper ledger. Right side: A clean, glowing uBank dashboard interface floating in a glass card.
*   **Call to Action (Banana Yellow)**: "Download uBank Today"

### **Poster 2: The "Growth" Pitch**
*   **Headline**: "Watch Your Commmunity Grow."
*   **Visual**: A close-up of a happy group looking at a phone, with a "Stats Card" graphic overlay showing a "Total Savings" graph going up.
*   **Copy**: "Automatic interest calculation. Instant loan approvals. Complete harmony."

### **Poster 3: The "Security" Pitch**
*   **Headline**: "Your Face is Your Key."
*   **Visual**: Large icon of the "Face Scan" or simple shield graphic in Emerald Green.
*   **Copy**: "Secure biometric login. Passkeys support. Your group's money has never been safer."

### **Poster 4: The "Treasurer's Friend"**
*   **Headline**: "Be the Super-Treasurer."
*   **Visual**: The "Record Cash" screen on a phone.
*   **Copy**: "Record contributions in seconds. Send receipts instantly. Spend less time counting, more time living."

## 4. Production Checklist
*   [ ] **Logo**: Ensure you have high-res vector (.SVG) versions of the uBank logo.
*   [ ] **Screenshots**: Take screenshots of the app in "Light Mode" for flyers (prints better) and "Dark Mode" for digital screens.
*   [ ] **QR Code**: Always include a QR code pointing to the download link.
